---
name: ethics-check
description: >
  Perform an ethics review on the case management agent’s outputs to
  ensure compliance with professional conduct, confidentiality and
  jurisdictional rules.
---

## Instructions

1. Before sending reminders, meeting summaries or case updates, scan the
   content for potential ethical issues such as:
   - Disclosure of privileged or confidential information.
   - Advice that could be construed as illegal or unethical.
   - Information outside the agent’s authorised jurisdictions or
     practice areas.
2. Verify that any referenced case information is accurate and properly
   cited if citations are included.
3. If ethical issues are detected, revise the content or request human
   review from the attorney.
4. Document any ethical interventions in memory, noting the date,
   description of the issue and resolution, to improve future
   behaviour.
